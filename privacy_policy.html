Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23/04/2024

Welcome to Litstructure. This Privacy Policy governs the collection, processing, and protection of your personal information when you use our platform and services. By using Litstructure services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you disagree, please refrain from using our services and close your account.

  1. 1. Our Commitment

    Litstructure.com, operated by Litstructure Technology Limited is committed to protecting your privacy rights and ensuring the confidentiality of your information. As an entity based in Kenya, we adhere to applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant legislation worldwide.

  2. 2. Collecting, Processing and Storage of Data

    Litstructure platforms collect and process personal data as necessary to provide our services. This includes information provided directly by you and data collected through automated means such as cookies and web beacons. Our data processing activities encompass various actions, including collection, recording, storage, alteration, disclosure, destruction, and blocking.

  3. 3. Personally Identifying Information (PII)

    Upon registration, we may request Personally Identifying Information (PII), such as your email address, to uniquely identify you. Additional details may be needed for transactions and personalized services. You can choose to share or withhold certain information in your public profile.

    Your personal data is securely stored within your user account, with restricted access via encrypted networks and permissions. No personal data can be downloaded or removed from the platform by anyone other than authorized personnel.

    You may engage in public interactions on the platform, such as posting comments or sharing content. Any shared content becomes publicly available, and by posting, you grant us a license to use and exploit it. We are not responsible for other users' use of publicly available information.

  4. 4. Course Information

    Upon registration, you can enroll in courses offered by Litstructure and affiliated publishers. Aggregated data on course enrollment may be shared with publishers for analysis and improvement purposes.

  5. 5. Third Party Information Processors and Service Providers

    We utilize third-party service providers for various functions, including cloud-based storage and payment processing. These providers adhere to industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data.

  6. 6. Technical Data Collection

    We collect technical data, including web server logs and device information, to analyze usage patterns and optimize our services. Cookies and web beacons are used to personalize your experience and track user sessions.

  7. 7. User Consent and Self-Service Control

    By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your data. You have access to self-service tools within your account to manage your information and privacy settings. You can also adjust browser settings to control cookies and tracking technologies.

  8. 8. Retention of Data

    We retain data for specified periods as required by law or for legitimate business purposes. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed, and user records are anonymized for statistical analysis.

  9. 9. Security and Disclosure of Data

    1. Robust Security: Although no system is entirely immune to attacks, we prioritize strong security measures to prevent unauthorized access and breaches.
    2. Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access personal data, with strict controls to prevent unauthorized changes or disclosures.
    3. Data Encryption: Personal data is encrypted for confidentiality and protection.
    4. Strict Time Limits: We delete data according to a strict schedule aligned with our retention policy.
    5. Logging: Detailed logs track data access and changes for transparency and accountability.
    6. Advanced Techniques: We use pseudonymization, anonymization, and encryption for added security.
    7. Restricted Database Access: Only authorized DevOps administrators can access databases, using multi-factor authentication.
    8. Regular Reviews: We regularly update security measures to adapt to new technology and risks.
    9. Employee Obligations: Employees must follow strict data protection principles and guidelines.
    10. User Responsibility: Users must maintain strong passwords and report suspected breaches promptly.
    11. Data Breach Response: If a breach occurs, affected individuals will be promptly notified, and authorities will be informed for monitoring and response.

  10. 10. Data Protection Officers

    Our Data Protection Officers oversee compliance with data protection laws and address privacy inquiries and concerns. You can contact them at dpo@litstructure.com.

  11. 11. Access Requests

    Users have the right to request access to your personal data and to update or delete it as necessary. Please use the self-service tools provided within your account or contact customer support for assistance.

  12. 12. Right to Object

    You may object to data processing activities that cause distress or inaccuracies in your personal data. We will address objections in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

  13. 13. International Transmission of Data

    We may transfer personal data across borders as necessary for service delivery. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during transmission.

  14. 14. Policy Review and Modification

    This policy is subject to periodic review and updates to reflect changes in laws and practices. Material updates will be communicated to users via email or platform notifications.

Thank you for entrusting Litstructure with your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at privacy@litstructure.com.
